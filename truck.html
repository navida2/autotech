Clean Truck Check Program

At Auto Tech Smog & Repair, we are licensed to perform Clean Truck Check emissions testing for diesel trucks. California requires periodic emissions inspections for medium- and heavy-duty trucks to ensure cleaner air and better performance.

HD I/M

We offer California's Heavy-Duty Vehicle Inspection and Maintenance (HD I/M) program, a regulation by the California Air Resources Board (CARB) that ensures heavy-duty vehicle emissions control systems function properly by requiring periodic testing. Only for diesel vehicles from 2013 or newer with a gross vehicle weight rating of 14,000 pounds or more

Heavy-Duty Vehicles

Our shop also tests trucks with a gross vehicle weight rating (GVWR) of 14,000 pounds or more. Stay compliant and avoid costly fines by keeping your truck inspection current.
